Overview

BoxPlz aims to collaborate with the different local/global companies to reduce trash. The company plans to connect with customers and collect good boxes from them. On the other end, the company will collaborate with local/global suppliers to ship collected boxes to them. Hence, the suppliers can take those boxes back and use again which can significantly reduce the trash as there is reduction in the manufacturing of extra boxes.
